David Miloschewsky

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Stony Brook University, advised by Supartha Podder. My research interests include quantum computation and computational complexity theory. Specifically, I am currently focusing on quantum query lower-bounding techniques and the analysis of various quantum complexity classes. Before beginning my PhD, I completed my B.A. in Mathematics and Computer Science at UC Santa Cruz, my M.S. in Applied Mathematics at Stony Brook University and worked as a software engineer.
